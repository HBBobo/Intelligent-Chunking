# PROBIOTIC

- A preparation containing live micro-organisms that can enter the GI ecosystem and produce positive health effects.

# PROBIOTIC

- A preparation containing live micro-organisms which, upon ingestion in sufficient numbers, exert health benefits beyond basic nutrition.

# PREBIOTIC

A substance or preparation which stimulates the growth of a potentially beneficial micro-organism(probiotic)

# SYNBIOTIC

A mixture of probiotic and prebiotic.
